# Easy Stuffed Bell Peppers

Original recipe: [https://cafedelites.com/crispy-chicken-parmesan/](https://www.food.com/recipe/easy-stuffed-bell-peppers-447201)

Servings: 6
Total time: 1hr 15 min.

## Indredients:
- 3 bell peppers
- 2 lbs ground beef (2 or 2.5 lbs)
- 1/2 medium sweet onion
- 1 cup uncooked rice
- 3 (10 3/4 ounce) cans tomato soup
- 1 (10 3/4 ounce) can water (from used tomato soup can)
- salt, to taste
- pepper, to taste
- garlic, to taste

## Instructions:
1. Cut and clean 3 bell peppers length wise from stem to butt. Remove seeds and stem and set aside.
2. Mix ground beef, onion, rice, and 1 can of tomato in large glass bowl. Season as desired with salt, pepper, garlic, etc --.
3. Pour 1 can of tomato soup into a deep baking pan and add 1 can of water. Mix together.
4. Fill each pepper 1/2 with ground beef until all the peppers are full and meat mixture is gone.
5. Place each stuffed pepper into pan. Use 1 can of tomato soup to top each pepper.
6. Cover with aluminum foil and bake at 450 degrees for approximately 1 hour or until peppers are soft.

 
 ## Notes:
  - You can also add cheese or other toppings to the top of the peppers.
  - If you don't have rice you can use Ritz crackers.
  - Some people like to add an egg to the meat mixture to help the meat stick together.
  - The original author likes to serve these with a side of rice, mashed potatoes, or noodles.
