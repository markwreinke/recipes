# James Custer's Grandma's Carrot Cake

Original recipe: 

Servings: 
Prep time:
Cook time: 
Total time: 

## Indredients:
### Cake:
- 1 cup oil
- 4 eggs
- 3 cups carrots, grated
- 2 cups sugar
- 2 tsp baking soda
- 2 tsp cinnamon
- 1 tsp salt
- 2 cups flour

### Frosting:
- 8 oz cream cheese
- 1 stick butter
- 1 box powdered sugar (yellow box, maybe a pound?)
- 1 cup nuts (pecans?)
- 2 tsp vanilla


## Instructions:
### Cake:
1. Cream together:
     - 1 cup oil
     - 4 eggs
     - 3 cups grated carrots
  
2. Add 2 cups sugar and stir again
3. In another bowl mix 2 tsp baking soda, 2 tsp cinnamon, 1 tsp salt, and 2 cups flour
4. Add dry and wet ingredients and mix well
5. Bake 350 degrees in 3 pans (set timer for 25 min and check with toothpick)

### Frosting:
1. Mix:
   - 8 oz cream cheese
   - 1 stick butter
   - Box of powdered sugar
   - 2 tsp vanilla
2. Top cake with nuts after applying frosting.
   
## Notes:
- This recipe is from James Custer at Auto-Owners. It was from his grandmother.
